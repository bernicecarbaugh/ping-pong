Ping Pong

An exercise to practice using Javascript arrays

Let's play a silly exercise!

Enter two numbers equal or greater than zero, with the second larger than the first.

I'll give you a range of all the numbers between the two but with these twists:

  • I'll replace numbers divisible by 3 with "ping"
  • I'll replace numbers divisible by 5 with "pong"
  • I'll replace numbers divisible by 15 with "ping pong"
